About Alex

Alex is a web developer, web strategist, and internet guy who lives in Omaha, Nebraska with his wife and children. He builds things with What Cheer and Pack.

He's also the vice president of Edison Elementary PTA, and is interested in using technology to create a better school experience for students, parents, teachers, and staff. Alex also really likes pushing the giant mop back and forth in the gym.

His wife, Jamie, is a recipe developer and much better blogger than her husband. She has been posting her delicious recipies online since 2007.
