Alex P. Gates is a web developer, strategist, and technophile based in Omaha, Nebraska.

As a partner at What Cheer, Alex acted as developer, strategist, and project manager on projects for Union Pacific Railroad, Saddle Creek Records, Woodmen of the World, MindMixer, Post Punk Kitchen, Silicon Prairie News, The US Department of Energy, and What Cheer's own projects, Hot or Cold, Bumble, Pep Rally, I Live in Omaha, and Selective Perspective Detective Objective.

At Pack, Alex is building a sophisticated email platform and oversees a community of over fifty Pack Leaders who curate highly engaging breed-specific newsletters every week.

He's also a two-time Startup Weekend Omaha winner, president of Edison Elementary PTA and serves on the board for Iowa Business Horizons, a weeklong summer program for high school students who want to learn about entrepreneurship and business.
