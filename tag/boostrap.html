About Alex

Alex is a web developer living in Omaha, Nebraska. He builds things with Pack and What Cheer.

He's also the vice president of Edison Elementary PTA, where he enjoys using technology to improve the lives of students, and occasionally pushing the giant mop back and forth in the gym.

His wife, Jamie, is a recipe developer and much better blogger than her husband. She has been posting her delicious recipies online since 2007.

Alex gets particularly excited about using data and code to make things better or more interesting. This includes fun and playful websites, powerful web applications, weather or voice-controlled light bulbs, telephone / sms apps, etc. As time allows, he promises to write more about fun things he's built since launching his first website in 1996.
