Alex P. Gates is a web developer, strategist, and technophile based in Omaha, Nebraska.

Alex is the community developer at Pack. That means he's constantly developing new campaigns, theories, email, and software for engaging Pack's members and visitors. It's a great combination of technology and humanity. Most recently, he's proudest of the work he's done on building a custom newsletter app for sending over 1M newsletters via Pack.

As a partner at What Cheer, Alex was all these things at once: web developer, strategist, business developer, and project manager on projects for MindMixer, The US Department of Energy, Silicon Prairie News, Union Pacific Railroad, Saddle Creek Records, Woodmen of the World, and Post Punk Kitchen. He also worked on What Cheer's own projects, Hot or Cold, Bumble, Pep Rally, I Live in Omaha, and Selective Perspective Detective Objective.

Alex is a two-time Startup Weekend Omaha winner, president of Edison Elementary PTA, and serves on the board for Iowa Business Horizons, a week-long summer program for high school students who want to learn about entrepreneurship and business.
